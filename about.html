Our Goal

To examine the trends of bike-share usage and look for solutions to enhance the bike-share contribution to all aspects of our lives.


Metro Bike-Share To understand bike-share program performance, uncover trends that help answer how it can be done better.
Current Users To better plan your bike trip with stations popularity and bike lanes info.
Everyone Else To understand the bike-sharing situation in Los Angeles, to know the benefits of bike-sharing, and cosider changing your commute!
